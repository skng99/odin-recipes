Pizza Dough

This is a simple and tasty overnight pizza dough. Pizza is one of the easiest things to make at home. You can put any toppings suiting your taste.

Ingredients

  • 153g 00 flour
  • 153g all purpose flour
  • 8g fine sea salt
  • 2g active dry yeast
  • 4g extra virgin olive oil
  • 220g luke warm water

Steps

  1. Combine the two flours and salt, mix well
  2. Create a small well and add 200g water, yeast and oil in the well
  3. Knead until well combined or aobut 3 minutes
  4. let mixture rest for 15 minutes
  5. Knead dough again for 3 minutes
  6. Cut into two equal pieces and shape into balls
  7. Place of a flour tray/surface; cover with damp cloth to let rise 3 to 4 hours at room temp or overnight in fridge
  8. to make pizza, please dough ball on lighly floured surface and stretch with your fingers
  9. Brush surface with oil; add favourite toppings
  10. Bake at 450 deg F for 10 to 15 minute
